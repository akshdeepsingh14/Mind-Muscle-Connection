Barbell Curl

How to Do

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart holding the barbell underhand (palms up).
  2. Keep elbows close to your torso and avoid swinging your body.
  3. Curl the bar up by contracting your biceps while keeping your back straight.
  4. Squeeze at the top for 1 second.
  5. Lower the bar slowly with full control to stretch the biceps.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 reps
  • Set 2: 10 reps
  • Set 3: 8 reps

Targets: Biceps brachii (main head), forearms

Benefits: Builds stronger and bigger arms, improves overall pulling strength
