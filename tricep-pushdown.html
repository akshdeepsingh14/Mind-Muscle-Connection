Tricep Pushdown

How to Do

  1. Grip the bar or rope attachment with elbows locked by your sides.
  2. Push the handle downward fully extending arms.
  3. Squeeze triceps at the bottom.
  4. Return slowly with control.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 reps
  • Set 2: 10 reps
  • Set 3: 8 reps

Targets: Tricep lateral head

Benefits: Improves arm strength and definition
