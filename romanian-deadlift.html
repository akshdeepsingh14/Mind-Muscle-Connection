Romanian Deadlift (RDL)

How to Do

  1. Stand with feet hip-width apart and hold a barbell in front of your thighs.
  2. Keep your back straight and push your hips backward.
  3. Lower the bar while feeling a stretch in your hamstrings.
  4. Return to standing by driving hips forward and squeezing glutes.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 reps
  • Set 2: 10 reps
  • Set 3: 8 reps

Targets: Hamstrings, glutes, lower back.

Benefits: Improves hamstring strength, glute growth & overall posterior chain.
