Preacher Curl

How to Do

  1. Sit on the preacher bench with chest and arms placed on the pad.
  2. Hold the barbell/dumbbell underhand with arms fully extended.
  3. Curl upward while keeping the upper arms fixed on the pad.
  4. Squeeze biceps hard at the top for 1 second.
  5. Lower slowly until arms are fully extended.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 reps
  • Set 2: 10 reps
  • Set 3: 8 reps

Targets: Biceps (short head)

Benefits: Maximum isolation, prevents cheating, builds bicep thickness
