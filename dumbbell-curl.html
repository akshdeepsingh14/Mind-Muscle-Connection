Dumbbell Curl

How to Do

  1. Stand straight with dumbbells in each hand, palms facing forward.
  2. Keep elbows fixed close to your torso.
  3. Curl the dumbbells up while squeezing your biceps.
  4. Pause at the top, then lower slowly under control.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 reps
  • Set 2: 10 reps
  • Set 3: 8 reps

Targets: Biceps (long + short head)

Benefits: Builds arm size, strength & peak shape
