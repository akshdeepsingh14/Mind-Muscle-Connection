Leg Extensions

How to Do

  1. Adjust the pad so it rests on your lower shins.
  2. Grip the handles and keep your back flat on the seat.
  3. Extend your legs fully while squeezing your quads.
  4. Slowly lower back down with control.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 15 reps
  • Set 2: 12 reps
  • Set 3: 10 reps

Targets: Quadriceps (front thighs).

Benefits: Great for quad isolation, enhances leg definition & knee strength.
