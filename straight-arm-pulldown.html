Straight Arm Pulldown

How to Do

  1. Stand facing the cable machine and grip the straight bar slightly wider than shoulder width.
  2. Keep your arms straight and core tight.
  3. Pull the bar down toward your hips by engaging your lats.
  4. Slowly raise it back up while keeping your arms straight.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 15 reps
  • Set 2: 12 reps
  • Set 3: 10 reps

Targets: Lats, Teres Major, Upper Back

Benefits: Improves back width, enhances mind-muscle connection for lat activation.
