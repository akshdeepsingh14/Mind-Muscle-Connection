Decline Bench Press

How to Do

  1. Lie on a decline bench securely with feet locked.
  2. Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder width.
  3. Lower the bar to the lower chest area.
  4. Push up while squeezing the chest.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 Reps
  • Set 2: 10 Reps
  • Set 3: 8 Reps

Targets: Lower Chest, Shoulders, Triceps

Benefits: Develops strong lower pecs and improves overall chest strength.
