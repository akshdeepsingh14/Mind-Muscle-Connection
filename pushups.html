Push-Ups

How to Do

  1. Place hands shoulder-width apart.
  2. Lower chest close to the ground.
  3. Push up while keeping your core tight.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 15 Reps
  • Set 2: 12 Reps
  • Set 3: 10 Reps

Targets: Chest, Shoulders, Triceps

Benefits: Strengthens entire upper body, improves muscle tone.
