Incline Bench Press

How to Do

  1. Set the bench to a 30–45° incline.
  2. Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder width.
  3. Lower the bar to the upper chest slowly.
  4. Push up while squeezing upper chest.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 Reps
  • Set 2: 10 Reps
  • Set 3: 8 Reps

Targets: Upper Chest, Shoulders, Triceps

Benefits: Builds upper chest thickness and strong pushing power.
