Leg Press

How to Do

  1. Sit on the leg press machine with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Push the platform to extend your legs without locking your knees.
  3. Lower the weight slowly to about 90Â° knee bend.
  4. Push back through your heels to the starting position.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 15 reps
  • Set 2: 12 reps
  • Set 3: 10 reps

Targets: Quads, glutes, hamstrings.

Benefits: Great for heavy leg training with less lower-back stress.
