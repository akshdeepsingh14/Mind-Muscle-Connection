Spider Curl

How to Do

  1. Lay chest-down on an incline bench keeping arms hanging straight.
  2. Hold dumbbells with a supinated grip (palms up).
  3. Curl the weights up while keeping elbows fixed.
  4. Pause and squeeze your biceps at the top.
  5. Lower slowly to full stretch.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 reps
  • Set 2: 10 reps
  • Set 3: 8 reps

Targets: Biceps peak, especially the long head

Benefits: Enhances bicep shape and isolation by removing body swing
