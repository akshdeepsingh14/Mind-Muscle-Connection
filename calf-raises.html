Calf Raises

How to Do

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Raise your heels as high as possible while squeezing the calves.
  3. Lower slowly and feel the stretch at the bottom.
  4. Keep your core tight and posture upright.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 20 reps
  • Set 2: 18 reps
  • Set 3: 15 reps

Targets: Gastrocnemius & Soleus (calf muscles).

Benefits: Improves lower-leg strength, balance & athletic performance.
