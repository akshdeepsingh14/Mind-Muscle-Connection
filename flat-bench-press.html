Flat Bench Press

How to Do

  1. Lie flat on the bench with your feet firmly planted.
  2. Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width.
  3. Lower the bar to mid-chest level under control.
  4. Push the bar upward until arms are fully extended.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 Reps
  • Set 2: 10 Reps
  • Set 3: 8 Reps

Targets: Middle Chest, Shoulders, Triceps

Benefits: Builds overall chest size & upper-body pressing power.
