Deadlift

How to Do

  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, barbell close to your shins.
  2. Grip the bar with both hands while keeping your back straight.
  3. Lift the bar by pushing through your legs and extending your hips.
  4. Lower the bar down under control while keeping your back neutral.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 10 reps
  • Set 2: 8 reps
  • Set 3: 6 reps

Targets: Lower Back, Glutes, Hamstrings, Traps, Core

Benefits: Builds total-body strength and improves posture & power.
