Pec Dec Fly

How to Do

  1. Sit on the pec deck machine and adjust the seat height.
  2. Place forearms on pads and hold handles firmly.
  3. Bring arms together in front of your chest.
  4. Squeeze and slowly return to starting position.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 15 Reps
  • Set 2: 12 Reps
  • Set 3: 10 Reps

Targets: Inner Chest, Chest Contraction

Benefits: Strongest chest squeeze & muscle activation.
