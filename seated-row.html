Seated Cable Row

How to Do

  1. Sit upright and place your feet firmly on the foot pad.
  2. Grip the handle with both hands and keep your back straight.
  3. Pull the handle towards your lower chest while squeezing your back.
  4. Slowly return your arms forward without rounding your back.
