T-Bar Rows

How to Do

  1. Stand over the bar with feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Bend your knees slightly and keep your back straight.
  3. Grab the handle and lift the bar to start position.
  4. Pull the bar towards your chest while squeezing shoulder blades.
  5. Slowly lower the weight back while controlling the movement.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 reps
  • Set 2: 10 reps
  • Set 3: 8 reps

Targets: Middle back, lats, rear delts, biceps.

Benefits: Increases back thickness, improves posture and pulling strength.
