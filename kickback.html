Tricep Dumbbell Kickback

How to Do

  1. Bend forward with a flat back and hold dumbbells.
  2. Keep elbows fixed behind you.
  3. Extend arms fully squeezing triceps.
  4. Lower slowly under control.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 reps
  • Set 2: 10 reps
  • Set 3: 10 reps

Targets: Triceps (lateral & long head)

Benefits: Sharpens muscle definition
