Barbell Squats

How to Do

  1. Place the barbell across your upper traps and stand with shoulder-width stance.
  2. Push hips back and bend knees to lower down keeping chest up.
  3. Squat until thighs are at least parallel to the floor.
  4. Push through heels and return to standing position.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 10 reps
  • Set 2: 8 reps
  • Set 3: 6 reps

Targets: Quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, lower back.

Benefits: Builds overall leg strength, improves power and stability.
