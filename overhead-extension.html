Overhead Tricep Extension

How to Do

  1. Hold a dumbbell overhead with both hands.
  2. Lower the weight behind your head by bending elbows.
  3. Extend arms fully to finish each rep.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 reps
  • Set 2: 10 reps
  • Set 3: 8 reps

Targets: Tricep long head

Benefits: Increases tricep thickness
