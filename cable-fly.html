Cable Fly

How to Do

  1. Stand in the middle of cable machine holding handles.
  2. Keep a slight bend in your elbows.
  3. Bring hands together in front of your chest.
  4. Squeeze chest and return slowly.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 15 Reps
  • Set 2: 12 Reps
  • Set 3: 10 Reps

Targets: Inner Chest, Shoulders

Benefits: Improves chest definition & stretch.
