Lateral Raises

How to Do

  1. Hold dumbbells by your side with slight elbow bend.
  2. Raise arms to shoulder height like wings.
  3. Lower slowly with control.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 15 reps
  • Set 2: 12 reps
  • Set 3: 10 reps

Targets: Side delts.

Benefits: Builds shoulder width and round delt shape.
