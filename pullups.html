Pull-Ups

How to Do

  1. Grab the pull-up bar with a shoulder-width grip.
  2. Hang with arms fully extended & engage your core.
  3. Pull yourself up until chin passes the bar.
  4. Lower slowly with controlled movement.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 10 Reps
  • Set 2: 8 Reps
  • Set 3: 6 Reps

Targets: Lats, Biceps, Upper Back

Benefits: Best exercise for wide & strong back.
