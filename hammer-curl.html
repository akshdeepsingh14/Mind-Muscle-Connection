Hammer Curl

How to Do

  1. Stand tall holding dumbbells with a neutral grip (palms facing each other).
  2. Keep elbows close to your body and avoid swinging.
  3. Curl the dumbbells upward using your biceps and brachialis.
  4. Squeeze at the top for 1 second.
  5. Lower the dumbbells slowly with full arm extension.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 reps
  • Set 2: 10 reps
  • Set 3: 8 reps

Targets: Brachialis, brachioradialis, biceps

Benefits: Increases arm thickness and forearm strength
