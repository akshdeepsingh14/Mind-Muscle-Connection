Close Grip Bench Press

How to Do

  1. Grip the bar slightly narrower than shoulder width.
  2. Lower bar to mid-chest keeping elbows tucked.
  3. Press back up squeezing triceps.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 10 reps
  • Set 2: 8 reps
  • Set 3: 6 reps

Targets: Triceps + chest support

Benefits: Strength booster for pressing movements
