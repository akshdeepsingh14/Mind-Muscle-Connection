Front Raises

How to Do

  1. Hold dumbbells at thighs with palms down.
  2. Raise one or both arms straight in front up to shoulder level.
  3. Lower back slow and controlled.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 reps
  • Set 2: 10 reps
  • Set 3: 8 reps

Targets: Front delts.

Benefits: Improves shoulder aesthetics and pressing strength.
