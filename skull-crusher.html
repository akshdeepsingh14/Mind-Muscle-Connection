Skull Crusher

How to Do

  1. Lie flat holding an EZ-bar above your chest.
  2. Lower the bar toward your forehead by bending elbows only.
  3. Extend arms back to starting position.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 reps
  • Set 2: 10 reps
  • Set 3: 8 reps

Targets: Long head of triceps

Benefits: Builds triceps size & strength
