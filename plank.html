Plank

How to Do

  1. Lie face down and lift your body on forearms and toes.
  2. Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  3. Engage your core and avoid letting hips drop or rise.
  4. Breathe steadily while holding the position.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 30–45 sec hold
  • Set 2: 45–60 sec hold
  • Set 3: Max hold

Targets: Core, Abs, Lower Back, Stabilizers.

Benefits: Builds core strength, posture, balance & reduces lower back strain.
