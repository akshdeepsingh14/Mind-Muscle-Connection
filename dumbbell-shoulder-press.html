Dumbbell Shoulder Press

How to Do

  1. Sit upright with dumbbells at shoulder height.
  2. Press dumbbells upward until arms are fully extended.
  3. Lower back to starting position under control.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 reps
  • Set 2: 10 reps
  • Set 3: 8 reps

Targets: Front and side delts, triceps.

Benefits: Builds shoulder size and overhead pressing strength.
