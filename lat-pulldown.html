Lat Pulldown (Cable)

How to Do

  1. Adjust the knee pad so your legs are locked under it.
  2. Grab the bar slightly wider than shoulder width (or your chosen grip).
  3. Lean back ~10–15° with a neutral spine and engaged core.
  4. Pull the bar down to your upper chest while driving elbows down and back.
  5. Pause and squeeze your lats at the bottom for 1 second.
  6. Slowly return to full arm extension, feeling the stretch in the lats.

Workout Plan

  • Set 1: 12 reps
  • Set 2: 10 reps
  • Set 3: 8 reps
  • Optional: Drop set on last set for extra volume

Targets: Latissimus dorsi (lats), upper back, biceps.

Benefits: Builds back width, improves pulling strength, and helps posture when performed with good form.
